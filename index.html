FAQ

Got Questions?

What kind of projects do you work on?+

I specialize in AI products, enterprise SaaS, fintech, healthcare, logistics, and government technology platforms — particularly Explainable AI dashboards and complex multi-role systems.

What's your design process?+

Research-first. I start with user interviews and journey mapping, move through information architecture and wireframing, then high-fidelity design with a focus on progressive disclosure and cognitive load reduction — validated through usability testing.

How long does a project usually take?+

It varies — from 1-month focused engagements to multi-month platform builds. My fintech dashboard ran 6 months; field-ops projects around 3 months.

Are you open to freelance work?+

Yes — I'm open to full-time roles, freelance projects, and consulting engagements across AI, enterprise SaaS, and complex systems.
